*Serves 8*

### Ingredients

- 1 dash Sugar
- 2 Eggs
- 3/4 cup Milk
- 2 Tbsp. Butter (melted)
- 1 cup Coconut
- 1 dash Vanilla
- 1 prepared Pie Shell

### Preparation

1. Mix all ingredients and pour into pie shell.
2. Bake at 300 for 45 minutes.
